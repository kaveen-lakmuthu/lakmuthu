A lightweight simulation engine for qubit states, gate transformations (Hadamard, CNOT, Pauli X/Y/Z), and superposition probability measurements.

* **Language**: C++ and Go
* **Core Concepts**: Complex vector spaces, matrix transformations, tensor products.

The project simulates quantum register actions locally on standard CPU hardware using high-efficiency vector calculations to inspect gates without physical hardware noise.
